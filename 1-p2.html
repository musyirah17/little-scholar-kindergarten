Little Islamic Scholar Program

Go to the tabbed menu to learn more about the Little Islamic Scholar Program's syllabus and sessions offered.

SYLLABUS

The Syllabus offered for the Little Islamic Scholar Program covers all aspects of academic and religion. The program is a comprehensive program to develop student in reading and writing in Bahasa Melayu and English, increase their interest in simple Mathematics and Science and blooming their creativity with Arts and Music classes as well. This program also incorporates Islamic studies along with stress-free classes with PE and Playtime sessions.

SUBJECTS

  • Iqra
  • Prayer**
  • Surahs & Duas
  • Reading & Writing in Bahasa Melayu(BM)
  • Reading & Writing in English(EN)
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Art
  • Music
  • Physical Education(PE)
  • Playtime

    • ** Only available for full day sessions
