About Us

Little Scholars Kindergarten is an institution that focuses on developing children’s intellectual and spiritual capabilities along with their creativity. Our programs not only focus on worldly aspects and knowledge, but are also very focused in improving children’s morals and Islamic knowledge. A well-balanced mixture of academic, religion and artistry sessions are held with ample breaks in between, so that children do not get overwhelmed with our syllabus.

Our objective in Little Scholars Kindergarten are as below:
  • To develop children in academic aspects such as reading in Bahasa Melayu, English and helping children in simple math.
  • To increase Muslim children’s knowledge within Islamic practices such as solat, du’a and reciting the iqra.
  • To expand children’s morality and individuality in teaching them moral values and proper manners.
  • To enhance children’s creativity with art classes and events like show-and-tells.
  • To help children instill fun learning in their lives.
