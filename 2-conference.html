Upcoming Event: Parent And Teacher Conference Day

Date: 11 January 2021
Venue: Little Scholar Classroom
Time : 10.00 a.m - 11.00 a.m

Parent And Teacher is an annual conference between the parents and teachers of students to discuss a child's progress at kindergarten and find solutions to academic or behavioral problems. Parent-teacher conferences supplement the information conveyed by report cards by focusing on students' specific strengths and weaknesses in individual subjects and generalizing the level of inter-curricular skills and competences. Student parents are welcomed to discuss about their children growth in terms of academic, creativity and communication in class.
The discussions include:

  • Child's performance in class during learning and teaching.
  • Child's growth in academic, morality and creativity during class and after class
  • Communication development between students, teachers and their classmates.
  • Child's behaviour in class and between their friends are good or need improvement.
