The Little Scholar Program

The Little Scholar Program follows a systematic teaching, comprehensive and creative syllabus that exposes children to exciting hands on activities and group participation. This program is designed to enhance children's creativity, intellect, mannerism and many more valuable aspects. It is also a program for children from age 3 to 6 years old which also prepers children for their primary school education.

The Little Islamic Scholar Program

The Little Islamic Scholar program is a comprehensive program that incorporates Islamic studies along with the subjects offered in the Little Scholar Program. This makes the Little Islamic Scholar Program a holistic program suited for Muslim Children. The prgram is designed for children from age 3 to 6 years old which also prepers children for their primary school education.
