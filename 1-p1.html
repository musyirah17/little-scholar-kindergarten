Little Scholar Program

Go to the tabbed menu to learn more about the Little Scholar Program's syllabus and sessions offered.

SYLLABUS

The Syllabus offered for the Little Scholar Program covers all aspects of academic. The program is a comprehensive program to develop student in reading and writing in Bahasa Melayu and English, increase their interest in simple Mathematics and Science and blooming their creativity with Arts and Music classes as well. The syllabus also incorporates stress-free classes with PE and Playtime sessions.

SUBJECTS

  • Reading & Writing in Bahasa Melayu(BM)
  • Reading & Writing in English(EN)
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Art
  • Music
  • Physical Education(PE)
  • Playtime
