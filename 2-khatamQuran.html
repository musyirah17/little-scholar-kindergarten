Upcoming Event: Khatam Quran Day

Date: 9 October 2020
Venue: Little Scholar Kindergarten Classroom
Time : 9.00 a.m - 12.30 p.m

Khatam Quran is an event for Muslim students in completing the 30 juz of Quran. The students recite from Surah At-Takassur until An-Nas altogether. After the event, each student will be given a certificate for completing the 30 juz of Quran.

The objectives of this event are:

  • To encourage the student to always recite and loving to recite Quran
  • To teach the students basic of reading the Quran
  • To teach them basic tajwid in Quran
