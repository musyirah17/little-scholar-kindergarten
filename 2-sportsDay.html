Upcoming Event: Sports Day

Date: 4 April 2021
Venue: Little Scholar Field
Time : 7.30 a.m - 1.00 p.m

Sports days is event staged by Little Scholar Kindergarten in which students participate in competitive sporting activities, often with the aim of winning trophies or prizes.In our kindergarten, we use a house system feature such as blue, yellow, red and green to represent the students groups; and they competing in sports program to become a champion.

Games that are played on Little Scholar Kindergarten include:

  • 50 metre sprint
  • Egg race
  • Spoon Race
  • Sack Race
  • Parent and child race

The champion, runner-up and 3rd place will receive medals and hampers.
