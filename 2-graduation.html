Upcoming Event: Graduation Day

Date: 5 December 2021
Venue: Kuala Lumpur City Hall
Time : 8.30 a.m - 2.00 p.m

Graduation Day is an event which conducted at Kuala Lumpur City Hall. It is a ceremony where are students will receive their scroll form for completing their kindergarten class. This event is started with the headmaster speech, the giving of the scrolls and lastly photography session.The Graduation is attended by parents and family of the students.

The Graduation Day tentative programs are:

  • Singing Negaraku
  • Headmaster speech
  • Students oath by student representative
  • Giving of the scrolls
  • Photography session
